    Twitter Flight School helps brands and agencies develop impactful marketing campaigns. When Twitter asked for a way to congratulate their Flight School graduates, we broke out our stop-motion toys and paired them up with cel animation.

    We had a blast from start to finish and feel like we pretty much ‘nailed it’! The party of work resulted in twenty looping tidbits of celebration for big internet high-fives to the Twitter Flight School scholars.

    Client: Twitter
    Producer: Ryan Brown

    Production Company: Slanted Studios
    Director: Michelle Higa Fox
    Producer: Nika Offenbac
    Art Director: Erin Kilkenny

    Stop-Motion Animator: Matt Christensen
    Compositing: Raymo Ventura
    Production Assistant: Avi Jacob, Jessica Kleinman
    Color: Erin Kilkenny

    Sound Design: Huma-Huma

    Special Thanks: Wayne Robins and Dan Savage
